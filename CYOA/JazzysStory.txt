# **Secrets of Beaver Lodge**

## **A Choose-Your-Own Adventure**

How to Read:

At the end of each section, choose what Beverly should do next and go to the page listed.

## **Page 1 — The Whispering Woods**

At the edge of a dense, whispering forest stood Beaver Lodge. Its windows glowed warmly, welcoming guests—but the woods beyond flickered with strange, dancing lights.

Beverly Beaver adjusted her pink glasses.

“Something isn’t right,” she whispered.

Her cousin Beatrice stood beside her, quiet… watchful.

What should Beverly do?

- Investigate the lights in the forest → Go to Page 2

- Stay inside and ask Beatrice questions → Go to Page 3

## **Page 2 — Into the Woods**

Beverly steps into the forest. The air hums softly.

A flicker of light darts between trees. Then—footprints. Glowing ones.

A shadow moves behind her…

What now?

- Follow the glowing footprints → Go to Page 4

- Run back to the lodge → Go to Page 5

## **Page 3 — Secrets Over Cocoa**

Inside the lodge, Beverly sits with Beatrice.

“You know something,” Beverly says.

Beatrice pauses… then sighs.

“The forest holds secrets. Old ones.”

Before she can say more—

The lights outside flicker wildly.

What now?

- Go outside together → Go to Page 2

- Search the lodge for clues → Go to Page 6

## **Page 4 — The Hidden Symbols**

Following the footprints, Beverly finds trees carved with strange symbols.

They glow faintly.

A voice behind her:

“You shouldn’t be here…”

It’s Wiley the Wolf, emerging from the shadows.

What should Beverly do?

- Talk to Wiley → Go to Page 7

- Hide and observe → Go to Page 8

## **Page 5 — Back to Safety… Or Not**

Beverly rushes back to the lodge—but something is off.

The path… is different.

The door is gone.

The forest has shifted.

What now?

- Call out for Beatrice → Go to Page 9

- Try to retrace steps → Go to Page 4

## **Page 6 — The Old Records**

Inside the lodge, Beverly finds old journals.

Drawings of the same symbols. Notes about a “crossing point.”

Suddenly—Beatrice appears behind her.

“You’ve found it,” she says quietly.

“I can’t hide it anymore.”

What should Beverly do?

- Demand the truth → Go to Page 10

- Promise to help her → Go to Page 11

## **Page 7 — Wiley’s Warning**

Wiley the Wolf tilts his head.

“The forest remembers,” he says.

“And it does not like being changed.”

He steps closer.

“Your lodge sits on something ancient.”

What now?

- Ask about the symbols → Go to Page 10

- Ignore the warning and continue exploring → Go to Page 12

## **Page 8 — Watching the Shadows**

Beverly hides behind a tree.

The symbols pulse… and the ground hums.

Then—shadowy figures flicker between trees.

Not quite real.

Not quite gone.

What now?

- Run → Go to Page 5

- Study the figures → Go to Page 13

## **Page 9 — Lost in the Forest (Ending)**

“Beatrice!” Beverly calls.

No answer.

The forest shifts again.

Paths vanish.

Lights surround her.

She is not alone anymore.

ENDING: The Forest Keeps Its Secrets

## **Page 10 — The Truth Revealed**

Beatrice (or Wiley) explains:

“This land is a crossing point—where ancient forest energy flows.”

“The expansion is disturbing it.”

The symbols?

They’re warnings.

What should Beverly do?

- Stop the campground expansion → Go to Page 14

- Continue anyway → Go to Page 12

## **Page 11 — Allies in Secret**

Beatrice nods.

“Then you must know… I am part of a society that protects this forest.”

Together, they plan.

Logic and magic.

Science and mystery.

What now?

- Investigate the forest together → Go to Page 13

- Focus on fixing the campground → Go to Page 14

## **Page 12 — The Disturbance (Ending)**

Construction continues.

The ground trembles.

The symbols burn bright.

Something awakens.

Something ancient.

Something watching.

ENDING: You Woke What Slept

## **Page 13 — Understanding the Forest**

Beverly studies the patterns.

The lights… follow paths.

The symbols… mark boundaries.

The forest isn’t attacking—

It’s communicating.

What should Beverly do?

- Redesign the campground → Go to Page 14

- Keep the secret hidden → Go to Page 15

## **Page 14 — Harmony (Good Ending)**

The campground is redesigned.

Paths respect the forest.

Certain areas remain untouched.

The strange occurrences fade…

But magic remains.

ENDING: Peace with the Forest

## **Page 15 — The Secret Lives On (Mystery Ending)**

The truth is hidden.

The lodge thrives.

But some paths remain closed.

Some lights still flicker.

And Beatrice?

She continues her watch.

ENDING: The Secret Endures
